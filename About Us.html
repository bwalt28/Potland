ABOUT US

Welcome to POTLAND – your trusted source for premium, top-shelf cannabis and cannabis-based medications in Portland. We take pride in delivering the finest selection of highly tested, quality-assured products at unbeatable prices every day. Our extensive menu caters to a wide range of needs, featuring meticulously tested strains, concentrates, edibles, and more. Whether you’re a first-time user or a seasoned enthusiast, our knowledgeable and friendly staff is here to guide you every step of the way. At POTLAND, we understand the importance of discretion and reliability. That’s why our professional delivery drivers are always punctual, discreet, and committed to providing you with a seamless experience from order to doorstep. Integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. POTLAND is dedicated to setting the gold standard in Portland's cannabis industry, ensuring that every member receives exceptional service and products that meet the highest standards. Discover the difference at POTLAND – where premium cannabis meets unbeatable service.

We started off as Portland's first ever delivery only dispensary. We have since grown into a storefront but still offer the same amazing delivery service. With a $50 minimum, free delivery to anywhere within Portland City limits. Unfortunately we cannot deliver to hotels or outside areas. Staying at a hotel? Comedown to our storefront!

We take pride in supplying high quality, top shelf cannabis products at the best prices to Portland every day. Our staff is abundantly knowledgeable and welcoming; our delivery drivers are punctual and discreet. We are dedicated to providing our customers with the best experience, products, and prices in the industry.
